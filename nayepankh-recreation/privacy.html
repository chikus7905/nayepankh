Privacy Policy

At NayePankh Foundation, we prioritize the protection of your personal information. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, and utilize details provided by our donors, volunteers, and website visitors.

1. Information Collected

We collect personal information such as Name, Email Address, Contact Number, and PAN details (required strictly for issuing 80G tax receipts) when you make a donation or fill out volunteer forms.

2. How We Use Information

The collected details are utilized strictly for:
• Generating and emailing donation tax exemption receipts.
• Sending monthly community progress reports.
• Confirming volunteer assignments and internships.

3. Data Security

We incorporate industry-standard security practices (SSL encryption) during transaction stages to guard your information against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. We never sell or share donor databases with external advertising networks.
