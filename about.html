Our Journey

About NayePankh Foundation

Empowering underprivileged dreams, distributing health and nutrition, and giving wings to tomorrow's leaders.

How it started

Born in a Time of Global Crisis

Initiated in 2020 during the peak of the COVID-19 pandemic, NayePankh Foundation was formed to combat the sudden hardships faced by daily wagers and slum dwellers.

Founder Prashant Shukla and a dedicated group of high school students observed the critical gap in basic necessities—such as healthy food and primary education—which was worsened due to continuous lockdown restrictions. They came together, pool-funding resources to cook and distribute meals.

Officially registered on March 28, 2021, the NGO has since evolved into one of the largest student-run social upliftment networks in India, supporting over 2,00,000 lives across Uttar Pradesh and other states.

Our Purpose

Vision & Core Values

Our Vision

To create a society where no child goes to bed hungry, every young mind has access to quality schooling, and marginalized mothers live with dignity, security and proper sanitation.

Empathy & Action

"Think Global, Act Local". We believe in immediate ground mobilization. Identifying community problems and applying student-driven volunteer campaigns directly in target slums.

Absolute Transparency

Holding government registration, 12A, and 80G tax certifications. We publish distribution reports and ensure donor contributions directly reach beneficiaries with proof.
