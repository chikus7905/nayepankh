Think Global, Act Local

Let's Revolutionize the Society Together

NayePankh Foundation is one of the biggest student-led organizations in India with operations in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and various other cities.

Founded by Prashant Shukla, we work day and night with a strong desire to make our society a better place. By doing everything in our power, we strive to break the cycle of poverty and offer hope to children and mothers.
